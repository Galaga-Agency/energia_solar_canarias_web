## 2. Brand Concept & Philosophy

### 2.1 The Name Deconstructed

The studio performed an etymological analysis of the brand name to ground the identity:

| Word | Origin | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| **Energía** | Latin *energīa* → Greek *enérgeia* (Aristotle, 4th c. BC) | "Force in action", "vigor", "internal work" — *en* (within) + *ergon* (work/action). Aristotle used it to describe the realization of a potential: when something passes from possibility to action. |
| **Solar** | Latin *sol, solis* — Indo-European root *\*sāwelo / \*sehwol-* | The central star of the planetary system. Also the Roman solar deity *Sol Invictus*. Root meaning: "to shine", warmth and vital light. |
| **Canarias** | Latin *Canariae Insulae* | "The islands of the dogs." Reference from Roman historian Pliny the Elder, who described large, powerful dogs on one of the islands (likely Gran Canaria). |

**Synthesis:** "The power of the sun, put into action, in the islands."

### 2.2 Mythological Anchor — Amaterasu

The brand draws on the myth of **Amaterasu**, the goddess of the sun in Japanese Shinto — the most important deity in Shintoism and ruler of Takamagahara (the celestial realm).

**Shinto philosophy adopted by the brand:**
- Living in harmony with nature
- Balance and respect
- Connection with the environment
- Acknowledging that the invisible also exists

**Brand manifesto line (from the PDF):**
> *"La luz como origen, el equilibrio como sistema, y la energía como orden."*  
> ("Light as origin, balance as system, and energy as order.")

### 2.3 Sector Analysis & Positioning

The studio identified the following weaknesses common across the solar energy sector (both international and local Canarian competitors):

- Generic visual identities (no differentiation)
- Energy treated as a product, not as an idea
- Brands with no real positioning
- Lack of brand narrative

**ESC's strategic opportunity:** position as a premium, philosophically-grounded brand — energy as a concept, not a commodity. The reference used internally is: "Apple vs. the rest of the market."

**Brand positioning pillars:**
1. Identity & Differentiation
2. Brand Value
3. Consistency & Coherence
4. Emotional Connection
5. Market Reach & Positioning

## 4. Typography

### 4.1 Typeface

**Acumin** — the sole typeface used across the entire identity.  
Co-designed by **Carol Twombly** and **Robert Slimbach** (Adobe Originals).  
It is a neo-grotesque sans-serif — clean, neutral, highly legible, with slight humanist warmth.

> Available via Adobe Fonts (requires Adobe Creative Cloud subscription).

### 4.2 Available Weights Used

| Weight | Usage |
|---|---|
| Thin | Decorative large text, vertical running text |
| Light | Long-form body copy |
| Regular | Default body text |
| Italic | Subline "Canarias" in the wordmark — key brand signature |
| Semibold | "Energía Solar" in the wordmark — primary brand name |
| Semibold Italic | Emphasis within body copy |
| Bold | Headlines, poster typography, strong statements |
| Bold Italic | Emphasis in headlines |
| ExtraBlack | Large-scale display text, maximum impact moments |

### 4.3 Wordmark Typography Rules

The brand name is always split into two typographic lines:

```
Energía Solar     ← Acumin Semibold (or Bold depending on variant), upright
Canarias          ← Acumin Semibold Italic (or Regular Italic), same or smaller size
```

The italic on "Canarias" is a deliberate stylistic choice — it adds movement and elegance, distinguishing the geographic anchor from the core product name.

**Color variations of the wordmark:**
- Full `#e4572c` (orange) on light backgrounds
- Full white on dark/orange backgrounds
- Full `#1c1c1a` (near black) on light backgrounds (monochrome variant)

## 5. The Brand Symbol — The Blob / Aura

This is the most distinctive and philosophically significant element of the identity.

### 5.1 Concept

The studio asked: *"What shape does energy have? Can energy be seen? How is energy transmitted? How is it represented?"*

Inspired by the murmuration of starlings (a photograph of a massive flock forming fluid, organic shapes in the sky), the symbol was developed as:

> *"Not a fixed form, but a consciousness in movement. A living system where energy transforms into form."*

It is an **organic, amoeba-like blob** — blurred, soft-edged, with no clean outline. It suggests warmth, radiation, presence. It is never exactly the same shape, which is intentional — the brand system is described as "breathing like a living system."

### 5.2 Visual Characteristics

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| **Shape** | Irregular, organic, rounded with no sharp corners — somewhere between a rounded square, a teardrop, and an amoeba |
| **Color** | `#e4572c` exclusively |
| **Edges** | Heavily blurred / feathered — no hard edge whatsoever |
| **Rendering style** | Radial gradient from solid `#e4572c` at center to fully transparent at edges — like a glowing ember or heat bloom |
| **Blur quality** | Gaussian blur — soft, uniform fade in all directions |
| **Background interaction** | Semi-transparent at edges, bleeds into whatever background it sits on |
| **Size** | Scales from very small (icon/stamp size) to full-bleed page takeover |
| **Rotation/orientation** | Freely rotated and stretched — no canonical orientation |

### 5.3 The Morphing Sequence (from PDF page 20)

The PDF shows 5 progressive states of the blob, left to right:

1. **Perfect circle** — fully round, compact, centered
2. **Rounded square-ish** — slightly squared off, still compact
3. **Irregular blob with protrusion** — organic, a "tail" or protrusion emerging to one side
4. **Wide organic blob** — wider than tall, like a flattened drop
5. **Crescent / arc shape** — strongly elongated into a curved, almost crescent-like form

This sequence represents energy transforming — from potential (sphere) to action (flowing, directional form).

### 5.4 CSS / SVG Implementation Reference

**CSS approach (static):**
```css
.esc-blob {
  width: 200px;
  height: 200px;
  background: radial-gradient(circle at 40% 45%, #e4572c 0%, rgba(228, 87, 44, 0.6) 35%, transparent 70%);
  border-radius: 60% 40% 70% 30% / 50% 60% 40% 55%;
  filter: blur(18px);
}
```

**Animated morphing blob (CSS keyframes):**
```css
@keyframes blob-morph {
  0%   { border-radius: 50% 50% 50% 50% / 50% 50% 50% 50%; }
  25%  { border-radius: 60% 40% 70% 30% / 50% 60% 40% 55%; }
  50%  { border-radius: 40% 60% 30% 70% / 60% 40% 55% 45%; }
  75%  { border-radius: 70% 30% 50% 50% / 40% 60% 50% 60%; }
  100% { border-radius: 50% 50% 50% 50% / 50% 50% 50% 50%; }
}

.esc-blob {
  animation: blob-morph 8s ease-in-out infinite;
  filter: blur(20px);
  background: radial-gradient(circle at 45% 50%, #e4572c, transparent 70%);
}
```


## 6. Logo System — Versions

Three official logo versions are documented in the PDF:

### Version A — Stamp / Icon variant
- Orange filled square (slightly rounded corners)
- White wordmark inside the square
- Blob aura radiating out from behind/around the square
- Used for: small format (business card corner, Instagram avatar, folder stamp)

### Version B — Horizontal / Inline variant
- Small blob symbol to the left
- Wordmark in orange to the right
- Both elements at similar visual weight
- Used for: medium-format applications, letterhead, website header

### Version C — Large Display variant
- Large blob symbol top-left (bigger than in Version B)
- Large wordmark in near-black (`#1c1c1a`)
- More spacious, editorial feel
- Used for: large-format display, posters, presentations

### Key rules observed:
- The blob always accompanies the wordmark — it is never absent
- The blob always sits to the **left** or **top-left** of the wordmark
- The blob never has a hard edge or outline
- Clear space around the logo should be at minimum = height of the "E" in "Energía"

## 7. Brand Voice & Copywriting

### Taglines visible in the PDF:
- **"Diseñamos tu independencia energética"** — primary campaign headline (We design your energy independence)
- **"El territorio que ya lo tiene todo"** — secondary (The territory that already has it all)
- **"Enciende otra forma de pensar"** — tertiary, more provocative (Light up another way of thinking)
- **"Es momento de encender otra forma de pensar"** — long form of the above
- **"Imagina un mundo donde la energía es limpia, renovable y accesible para todos. Con Energía Solar Canarias, ese futuro es hoy."** — brand manifesto paragraph

### Tone of voice:
- Philosophical, not technical
- Warm but precise
- Premium without being cold
- References to nature, light, territory, and transformation
- Avoids: solar panel specs, percentage savings, typical energy company jargon
- Italic used for emotional/aspirational words (*independencia*, *Canarias*, *todo*, *hoy*)

## 10. Photography Direction

Based on mockup imagery throughout the PDF:

- **Landscapes:** Canarian terrain — volcanic rock, lanzarotean craters, desert dunes, cliffs, ocean
- **Aerial:** Drone shots of beaches (turquoise water + golden sand)
- **Sky:** Sunrise/sunset, golden hour, dramatic cloud formations
- **Space:** Earth from orbit with city lights (used in the "De lo amplio a lo esencial" section)
- **Texture:** Black volcanic sand close-up, natural surfaces
- **Nature:** Canarian cacti (cardones), palm trees, lush green hills

**Color grading of photography:**
- Warm golden tones for exteriors/sky
- High contrast, slightly desaturated for landscape/rock
- Never cold, never blue-tinted
- The orange blob is frequently composited directly over photography as a translucent overlay

```
  --esc-orange:      #e4572c   (primary, blobs, CTAs, logo highlight)

  --esc-black:       #1c1c1a   (text on light backgrounds)

LOGO: Wordmark only — "Energía Solar" (semibold) + "Canarias" (semibold italic)
      Always accompanied by the blob symbol to the left

BLOB SYMBOL:
  - Organic, amoeba-like shape
  - Color: #e4572c only
  - Edges: gaussian blur, no hard edge, fully transparent at periphery
  - Rendered as: radial-gradient + CSS blur, or SVG feTurbulence + feGaussianBlur

VOICE: philosophical, premium, nature-rooted, emotional
TAGLINE: "Diseñamos tu independencia energética"
SECTOR: Solar energy / Canary Islands / B2C + B2B
```
